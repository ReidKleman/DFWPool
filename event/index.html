DFW Pool Patrol Workshop

Practical, no-fluff training for pool owners and property managers across the Dallas–Fort Worth area. Learn maintenance basics, water chemistry, and equipment best practices from seasoned pros.

Reserve Your Spot

What You’ll Learn

  • Weekly care workflow: skimming, brushing, vacuuming, filters
  • Water chemistry essentials: pH, chlorine, alkalinity, hardness
  • Common issues and quick fixes: algae, cloudiness, staining
  • Equipment 101: pumps, heaters, automation & troubleshooting
  • Seasonal prep and safety checks
