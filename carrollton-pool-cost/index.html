Carrollton Pool Electricity & Utility Costs - Complete Breakdown

Pool electricity costs in Carrollton depend on your pump type and local electric rates. Here's what you need to know about running a pool in the DFW electric market.

Pool Pump Electricity Costs in Carrollton

The biggest ongoing electric expense for your pool is the pump. Based on current DFW electric rates (9.0-9.6¬¢/kWh), here are real monthly costs:

  • Variable Speed Pump (250W, 12 hrs/day): ~$11/month
  • Single Speed Pump (1000W, 8 hrs/day): ~$29/month
  • Large Single Speed (1500W, 6 hrs/day): ~$26/month

Pro tip: Variable speed pumps cost more upfront but save $200-300/year in electricity. The payback period is typically 2-3 years.

  • Base: $16.40/month (includes first 2,000 gallons)
