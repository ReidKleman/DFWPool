Pool Leak Detection in Dallas Fort Worth: The Professional Bucket Test Method

The bucket test is the gold standard for Dallas Fort Worth pool owners to distinguish between normal evaporation and pool leaks. This professional pool maintenance technique compares evaporation rates and helps determine when to contact DFW pool service professionals for leak detection and pool repair.

Step-by-Step Bucket Test Procedure

  1. Fill a 5-gallon bucket with pool water to within 2-3 inches of the rim
  2. Place the bucket on your pool step or secure it with a float
  3. Mark water levels both inside the bucket and on the pool wall
  4. Turn off pool equipment and wait 24-48 hours
  5. Measure water loss in both the bucket and pool
  6. Compare results to determine if a leak exists

DFW Pool Service: Interpreting Bucket Test Results

  • Normal Pool Evaporation: Pool and bucket lose equal water amounts - typical for Dallas Fort Worth climate
  • Pool Leak Detected: Pool loses more than 1/4 inch beyond bucket in 24 hours - requires professional pool repair
  • Pool Equipment Issues: Significant differences may indicate pump, filter, or plumbing problems requiring DFW pool service
