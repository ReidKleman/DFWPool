Complete Weekly Pool Maintenance Service

Your weekly pool cleaning service includes everything needed to maintain a healthy, sparkling pool. Our DFW professionals follow a comprehensive checklist to ensure nothing is missed, giving you peace of mind and perfect water every week.

Surface & Interior Cleaning

  • Surface skimming - removal of leaves, debris, and insects
  • Complete pool vacuuming - floor and walls
  • Brushing tile line and pool surfaces to prevent algae buildup
  • Cleaning waterline and removing buildup
  • Removal of large debris and foreign objects
  • Steps and bench cleaning and scrubbing

Water Chemistry & Treatment

  • Complete water testing - pH, chlorine, alkalinity, hardness
  • Chemical balancing and adjustment as needed
  • Chlorine level optimization for safe swimming
  • pH adjustment to prevent equipment damage
  • Shock treatment when required
  • Algae prevention treatment application

Equipment Maintenance

  • Empty and clean skimmer baskets
  • Empty and clean pump basket
  • Check and clean filter system
  • Inspect pump and equipment operation
  • Monitor water circulation and flow
  • Basic equipment troubleshooting and alerts

Additional Services

  • Water level adjustment and topping off
  • Pool area cleanup and maintenance
  • Equipment area cleaning and organization
  • Service report and chemical usage tracking
  • Seasonal maintenance recommendations
  • Problem identification and repair referrals
